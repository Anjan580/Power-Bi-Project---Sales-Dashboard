
# Super Store Sales Dashboard

##  Overview
The **Super Store Sales Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization project designed to analyze and monitor sales performance across multiple dimensions such as region, category, sub-category, payment mode, and customer segment.  
It provides clear insights into key metrics like total sales, profit, quantity sold, and monthly performance trends.

---

##  Objectives
- Track **overall sales, profit, and quantity** in real-time.  
- Understand **sales patterns by region, category, and sub-category**.  
- Identify the **most profitable segments** and **payment modes**.  
- Analyze **year-over-year (YOY)** trends for both sales and profit.  
- Support data-driven decision-making for business growth.

---

##  Key Insights
- **Total Sales:** 1.6M  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 22K  
- **Total Profit:** 175K  
- **Average Delivery Days:** 4  
- **Top Segment:** Consumer (48%)  
- **Top Category:** Office Supplies (0.64M sales)  
- **Top Sub-Category:** Chairs (0.18M sales)  
- **Most Used Payment Mode:** Cash on Delivery (43%)

---

##  Dashboard Components
### 1. **Filters**
- Region Selector: Central, East, South, West  

### 2. **KPIs**
- Sales  
- Quantity  
- Profit  
- Avg Delivery  

### 3. **Visuals**
- **Donut Charts**
  - Sales by Payment Mode  
  - Sales by Segment  
- **Bar Charts**
  - Sales by Category  
  - Sales by Sub-Category  
- **Line Charts**
  - Monthly Sales by YOY  
  - Monthly Profit by YOY  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** (for dashboard creation and data visualization)  
- **Excel / CSV Dataset** (data source)  
- **DAX** (for calculated measures and KPIs)

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset includes the following columns:
- Order ID, Product ID, Category, Sub-Category  
- Segment, Region, Sales, Profit, Quantity  
- Order Date, Ship Mode, Payment Mode
